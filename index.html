Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

APEX Training ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

APEX Training collects the following information to provide and improve our services:

  • Profile information you provide during onboarding, including your name, age, height, weight, fitness goals, experience level, available equipment, and any health conditions you choose to disclose
  • Workout data and training history generated through your use of the App
  • AI chat conversations related to fitness coaching
  • Subscription and purchase information processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate personalized AI-powered workout programs and recommendations
  • Provide AI fitness coaching through in-app chat
  • Track your training progress and provide performance insights
  • Process subscription payments
  • Improve the App's features and user experience
  • Respond to support inquiries

3. Data Storage

Your workout data, profile information, and chat history are stored locally on your device using on-device storage. We do not maintain external databases of your personal information. Subscription data is managed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. AI-Generated Content

The App uses artificial intelligence to generate workout recommendations, training schedules, and fitness coaching responses. Information you provide (such as your fitness goals, experience level, health conditions, and chat messages) may be sent to AI service providers to generate personalized content. This data is used solely for generating your responses and is not stored by these providers for other purposes beyond what is necessary to provide the service.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may integrate with the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — for subscription and in-app purchase management
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store — for payment processing
  • AI Service Providers — for generating personalized workout content and coaching

These services have their own privacy policies governing how they handle your data. We encourage you to review their respective policies.

6. Health & Fitness Data

APEX Training collects health and fitness-related information (such as your weight, height, fitness level, and health conditions) solely to personalize your workout experience. This data is stored locally on your device and is not shared with third parties for purposes unrelated to the App's core functionality. We treat all health-related data with the highest level of care.

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device for as long as the App is installed. If you delete the App, all locally stored data — including your profile, workout history, and chat history — will be permanently removed. Subscription records are maintained by Apple or Google per their respective retention policies.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information stored within the App
  • Request deletion of your data by resetting your profile within the App or by uninstalling the App
  • Opt out of data collection by discontinuing use of the App

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA) or California, you may have additional rights under GDPR or CCPA, including the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

10. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Since your data is stored locally on your device, its security is also dependent on the security of your device. We recommend using a passcode or biometric lock on your device to protect your data.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
