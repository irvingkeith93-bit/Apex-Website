Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Apex Training is owned and operated by Irving Industries LLC.

APEX Training ("we," "our," or "the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App may access, how that information is used, and how it is protected when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

APEX Training may access the following types of information to provide and improve the App:

  • Profile information you provide, such as your name, age, height, weight, fitness goals, experience level, available equipment, and any health-related details you choose to enter
  • Workout and training data generated through your use of the App
  • AI chat inputs and responses used for training guidance and support within the App
  • Subscription and purchase information processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store
  • Health and fitness data you explicitly authorize through Apple HealthKit, if you choose to connect it

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information accessed through the App to:

  • Generate personalized training plans, workout recommendations, and coaching guidance
  • Track workout progress and performance over time
  • Enable app features that rely on your profile, workout history, and preferences
  • Provide AI-assisted fitness support inside the App
  • Process subscriptions and manage account-related support requests
  • Improve the App's functionality and user experience

3. On-Device Data Storage

Your profile information, workout history, settings, and chat-related data are intended to be stored locally on your device. We do not maintain a separate external database of your personal fitness information for marketing purposes. If data storage practices change in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

4. HealthKit and Health Data

If you choose to connect Apple HealthKit, APEX Training may read and/or write health and workout data only with your explicit permission. Health data accessed through HealthKit is used solely to support fitness-related features inside the App, such as workout tracking, workout history, performance insights, and personalized recommendations.

Health data accessed through HealthKit is not used for advertising, marketing, or data broker purposes. To the extent supported by the App's implementation, HealthKit data is processed on-device and is not stored or shared outside your device except as necessary to provide the specific feature you choose to use.

5. AI-Generated Content

The App may use artificial intelligence to generate workouts, coaching suggestions, schedules, and support responses. Information you provide, including workout context, goals, preferences, and chat messages, may be processed for the sole purpose of generating relevant in-app responses and recommendations.

6. Third-Party Services

The App may rely on third-party services such as:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store — for payments and subscription processing
  • RevenueCat — for subscription and purchase management
  • AI Service Providers — for generating workout recommendations, coaching responses, or related in-app assistance
  • Apple HealthKit — for authorized health and workout data integration

These providers operate under their own terms and privacy practices. We encourage you to review their policies where applicable.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We do not share your personal or health information with third parties for advertising purposes. Information is only used as necessary to operate the App, process purchases, deliver requested features, or comply with legal obligations.

8. Data Retention

Data stored locally on your device remains there for as long as you keep the App installed, unless you remove it within the App or uninstall the App. If you delete the App, locally stored data may be removed from your device. Subscription records may continue to be maintained by Apple, Google, or other payment providers in accordance with their own policies.

9. Your Choices and Rights

You may have the ability to:

  • Review and update information stored within the App
  • Delete locally stored data by resetting the App or uninstalling it
  • Disconnect HealthKit permissions at any time in your device settings
  • Stop using the App at any time

Depending on where you live, you may also have additional privacy rights under applicable law, including rights related to access, correction, deletion, or restriction of personal data.

10. Children's Privacy

APEX Training is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that personal information from a child under 13 has been provided through the App, we will take appropriate steps to address it.

11. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information used by the App. Because much of the App's information may be stored locally on your device, protecting your device with a passcode, Face ID, Touch ID, or other security controls is important.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after any changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
